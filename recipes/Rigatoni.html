Baked Rigatoni

This baked rigatoni casserole, made with a flavorsome meat sauce loaded with Italian sausage, fresh herbs, and tomatoes, is a comforting cheesy baked pasta dish perfect for colder weather and always a crowd-pleaser/p>

Ingredients

  • Fresh Basil
  • Pasta
  • Mince meat
  • Olive oil
  • Mozarella
  • Tomatoes
  • onions
  • Aluminium foil

steps

  1. heat oil in pan on medium heat
  2. Add the mince meta
  3. Turn untill brown
  4. Add onions and then fresh tomatoes
  5. Add Pasta into same pot
  6. Let it all simer until the pasta is nearly cooked
  7. Transfer pot contents into casserole dish
  8. Top with cheese and wrap the dish with Aluminium foil
  9. Place in Oven for 20 minutes at 180 degrees celcius
